Top Eats Around TIU

  1. Ramen Bankara Ikebukuro Higashiguchi

    2. Address: 70-0013 Tokyo, Toshima City, Higashiikebukuro, 1 Chome−13−4 第一坂田ビル １F

    Ramen Bankara Ikebukuro Higashiguchi isn't just another ramen shop; it's a portal to pure ramen bliss. From the moment you step inside, the aroma of their rich, simmered broth hits you, promising a flavor explosion to come. Their ramen isn't just about the soup, though. The noodles are cooked to an ideal texture, offering a satisfying bite that complements the broth perfectly.

  2. Tully's Coffee Ikebukuro Sunshine City Alpa Store

    3. Address: 456 Sunshine Avenue, Tokyo, Japan

    This cozy cafe is a great spot to relax and enjoy a cup of coffee or tea. Tully's boasts a diverse coffee menu, catering to every taste bud. Whether you crave a classic espresso or a more adventurous seasonal latte, their skilled baristas will craft your perfect cup. Beyond coffee, they offer a selection of teas, from fragrant green teas to soothing herbal blends.
